Project Idea

Through other pages like the catalog, book details page, and purchase and confirmation processes, it becomes clear that "Book Haven" is a comprehensive online bookstore project that allows users to search for books, read their details, and complete purchases via user accounts, with a dedicated admin panel to manage inventory and orders.

  • Wide Collection Thousands of titles across multiple categories: Literature, Fiction, History, Self-development, and more.
  • User Experience User login/registration, detailed book pages, and an easy-to-use shopping cart.
  • Management & Monitoring Admin interface to add/edit books and track orders.
  • Payment & Delivery Secure payment methods and options for shipping and order fulfillment (shown in purchase confirmation pages).
